Privacy Policy

LingRise  ·  Last updated: May 14, 2025

LingRise ("we", "our", or "us") is an independent AI-powered speaking practice application for IELTS and TOEFL preparation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the LingRise mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information solely to provide the core features of the app:

  • Voice recordings: When you record a speaking response, your audio is temporarily processed to generate a transcript and feedback. Audio is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Speech transcripts: Text transcripts generated from your recordings are used to produce AI feedback and are not retained after your session ends.
  • Usage data: Basic, non-identifiable usage information (e.g., which exam type was selected) may be logged for debugging and improving the app.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information. No user account is required to use LingRise.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe your spoken responses and generate personalised feedback.
  • To display an AI avatar that delivers instructions and feedback within the app.
  • To improve the reliability and quality of the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the app's core functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

LingRise uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • HeyGen LiveAvatar — provides the real-time AI avatar video stream. Voice and avatar session data are processed by HeyGen in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • DeepSeek AI — generates spoken feedback based on your transcript. Transcripts are sent to DeepSeek's API and processed according to their privacy policy.
  • iFlytek (科大讯飞) — performs speech-to-text transcription of your recorded responses.

Each third-party service is bound by its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them independently.

4. Data Retention

LingRise does not maintain a user database. Voice recordings and transcripts are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of a single practice session. Once your session ends, your audio and transcript data are discarded.

5. Children's Privacy

LingRise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights

Because LingRise does not collect or store personal data linked to your identity, there is no personal profile to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data processed by our third-party providers, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or Canada, you may have additional rights under GDPR or PIPEDA. Please contact us with any questions.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit, including HTTPS encryption for all communications between the app and our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
